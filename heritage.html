Heritage of Patiala

Qila Mubarak

Qila Mubarak stands proud as a timeless symbol of royal grandeur with rich Punjabi heritage. it's majestic walls echo tales of bravery, culture and the legacy of Patiala's kings

Moti Bagh Palace

Moti Bagh Palace reflects the elegance and artistic taste of Patiala’s royal past. Surrounded by lush gardens.

Sheesh Mahal

Sheesh Mahal shines with intricate mirror work that reflects royal luxury at its finest. Its dazzling interiors turn light into art, telling stories of elegance and craftsmanship.

Baradari Gardens

Baradari Gardens offers a peaceful escape with its lush greenery and royal charm. Once a retreat for kings, it now invites visitors to relax amidst history and nature.

Ranbas

Once the royal guesthouse of Patiala, it reflects elegance with a touch of old-world charm. Today, its beautifully restored structure blends heritage with a calm, welcoming vibe.
